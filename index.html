Who We Are

Beautiful Spaces, Real Life

We believe your home should be as beautiful as it is livable. At Element321, we design interiors that reflect who you are and how you actually live. No showrooms. No untouchable rooms. Just thoughtful, layered spaces where families gather, memories happen, and every detail feels intentional.

Intentional

Every choice serves a purpose. We design with care, ensuring each element earns its place.

Personal

Your home is uniquely yours. We listen closely to craft spaces that reflect your family's story.

Livable

Beauty that works in real life. Spaces built for morning coffee, family dinners, and everything between.

HS

Heather Smith

Founder & Principal Designer

With a deep love for design and an eye for what makes a space feel like home, Heather founded Element321 to bring thoughtful, livable interiors to Central Texas families. She leads every project with warmth, precision, and a commitment to getting the details right. When she's not sourcing the perfect piece, she's at home in Georgetown with her husband and two daughters.

RM

Rachel Mazur

Designer & Brand Manager

A native Austinite with an instinct for style and storytelling, Rachel brings a keen creative vision to every project. She manages the Element321 brand and works alongside Heather to deliver cohesive designs that feel effortless and elevated. A mom of two, Rachel draws constant inspiration from how real families live and what they need from their spaces.

What We Do

Our Services

From a single room to an entire home, we bring clarity, beauty, and intention to every project.

Full-Service Interior Design

Comprehensive design from concept through installation. We handle space planning, sourcing, procurement, and styling so you can enjoy the transformation without the stress.

Room Refresh

Ready for a change but not a full renovation? We reimagine your existing space with updated furnishings, color, and styling that make it feel brand new.

New Construction

Building from scratch? We partner with you and your builder from the earliest stages to select finishes, fixtures, and furnishings that create a cohesive, move-in-ready home.

Renovation Design

Renovating requires a vision for how everything comes together. We guide the design direction through every decision, from layout changes to the finishing touches.
