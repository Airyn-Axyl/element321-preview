How Billing Works

We typically structure payments in phases: an initial design retainer, a midpoint payment at selection approval, and a final payment before installation. Furnishing and material costs are billed separately as items are ordered.

We're transparent about costs from the start. You'll always know what to expect before we move forward, and there are never surprise fees.

Inspiration Homework

Before we dive into design, we'd love to see what speaks to you. This helps us understand your taste faster and ensures we're aligned from the start.

Your Assignment

  • ✓ Collect 10+ inspiration photos from Houzz, Pinterest, Instagram, magazines, or anywhere you find beautiful spaces. Don't overthink it. If you love it, save it.
  • ✓ Follow @element321design on Instagram to see our latest projects and behind-the-scenes process. We share a lot of design thinking in our Stories.
  • ✓ Follow Element321 on Pinterest and browse our curated boards. Feel free to save anything that catches your eye to your own boards.
  • ✓ Note what you want to feel in each room. Cozy? Bright? Calm? Energized? The emotional quality matters as much as the visual style.
  • ✓ Think about how you use each space. Where does the family hang out most? Where do you work? Where do the kids do homework? Where does the dog sleep?
  • ✓ Identify any must-haves and dealbreakers. Is there a family heirloom that stays? A color you absolutely can't stand? A material you've always dreamed of? Tell us everything.
